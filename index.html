About Our Project

Financial opacity weakens trust. Our system ensures that every transaction, from government budgets to NGO donations and institutional fees, is traceable, authentic, and easy to understand. By providing role-based dashboards, proof uploads with GPS verification, and transparent visualizations, we aim to empower citizens, students, parents, and donors with clarity about how money truly flows.
